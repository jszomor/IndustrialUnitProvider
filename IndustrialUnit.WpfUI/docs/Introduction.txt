Welcome to Indsutrial Unit Manager!

The purpose of this software above the seek of my learning is to demonstrate a valuable
solution for customers who are seeking uniqely developed industry specific wide range 
of product list with the most important information for further usage.
This software gives a possiblity to user to maintain its own products or even creates a new database
to start over to separate lists by manufacturers or item names.
By further development it is possible the have features like pricing, engineering sizing,
order list, pre-configured group selection based on engineering calculation.
The industries which could be intrested in such solution are wastewater treatment, water treatment, energy, oil, food,
pharmaceutical. These fields have its own specific equipments however there are many
kind of product in common like pumps, valves and instruments.
